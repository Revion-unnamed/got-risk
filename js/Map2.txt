
## Map Structure: Regions & Territories

The board has **9 regions** (continents in standard Risk). Each grants a bonus army if fully controlled.

| Region | Bonus | Territories | Color on map |
|---|---|---|---|
| The North | 5 | Bear Island, Karhold, The Gift, Skagos, Wolfswood, Winterfell, The Dreadfort, Stony Shore, Barrowlands, White Harbor, Widow's Watch | Grey-green |
| The Vale of Arryn | 1 | The Fingers, The Eyrie, Gulltown | Teal/green |
| The Riverlands | 2 | The Twins, Pyke*, Harlaw*, Riverrun, Harrenhal, Mountains of the Moon, The Trident | Blue |
| The Iron Islands | 1 | Pyke, Harlaw, (2–3 small islands) | Gold/yellow |
| The Westerlands | 2 | Casterly Rock, Easterly Rock, Golden Tooth, Silverhill, Oxcross, Crakehall, Searoad Marshes | Dark red/maroon |
| The Crownlands | 2 | King's Landing, Cracklaw Point, Dragonstone, Kingswood, Blackwater Rush | Gold |
| The Stormlands | 1 | Storm's End, Tarth, Rainwood, Dornish Marches | Olive/tan |
| The Reach | 4 | Highgarden, Oldtown, Three Towers, The Arbor, Red Mountains, The Mander | Green-grey |
| Dorne | 1 | Sunspear, Sandstone, Greenblood | Orange-brown |

## Territory Connections (Adjacency)

> ⚠️ **These coordinates are approximate.** After placing the image, visually verify each circle aligns with the territory label on the map. Adjust cx/cy values as needed — expect to fine-tune ~10px on most territories.

```js
function renderTroopMarker(territory, count, ownerColor) {
  return `
    <g class="troop-marker" data-id="${territory.id}">
      <!-- Tap target (invisible) -->
      <circle 
        cx="${territory.cx}" cy="${territory.cy}" r="18"
        fill="${regionColors[territory.region]}" 
        fill-opacity="0"
        stroke="none"
        class="territory-hit"
        data-id="${territory.id}"
      />
      <!-- Troop badge -->
      <circle cx="${territory.cx}" cy="${territory.cy}" r="11" 
        fill="${ownerColor}" stroke="white" stroke-width="1.5"/>
      <text x="${territory.cx}" y="${territory.cy + 4}" 
        text-anchor="middle" font-size="10" 
        fill="white" font-weight="bold" 
        font-family="sans-serif">${count}</text>
    </g>
  `;
}
```

## Notes

- The map scrolls vertically (535px tall on a 360px screen) — ensure `overflow-y: scroll` on the container or page
- Sea connections (Dragonstone ↔ Cracklaw Point, Tarth ↔ Storm's End, Iron Islands ↔ coast) are valid attack routes — flag them with `sea: true` in your adjacency list if your game differentiates
- The Reinforcement Chart in the bottom-left corner of the image (showing troop icons) can be hidden behind a UI panel or cropped if distracting
